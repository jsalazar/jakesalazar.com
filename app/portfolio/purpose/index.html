Why
Education?

More than 100,000 classrooms are being staffed by instructors who are unqualified for their jobs.

Students who have highly qualified teachers three years in a row score as much as 50 percentile points higher than those who don’t.

Education is the next frontier in social justice.

Why the
University of Michigan?

Over 90% of our alumni have a job in their field within the first 6 months after graduating.

U-M is widely regarded as a national leader in teacher education, currently ranked 2nd in both elementary and secondary teacher education by U.S. News and World Report.

Our students learn to do the work of teaching in K-12 classrooms throughout the year. We offer them personal attention and mentorship to ensure their success.

A U-M master’s degree positions you for leadership roles and education careers beyond the classroom.
