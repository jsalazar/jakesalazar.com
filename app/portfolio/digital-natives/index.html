DIGITAL NATIVES

New Media and New Literacies

In classrooms and other learning environments, new media are expanding the notions of learning and literacy. Students examine contemporary research on learning technologies as well as the processes and practices through which people produce, understand, and use information.

INNOVATORS

Educational Studies and MBA (Dual Degree)

The MA/MBA program is designed for innovators who want to integrate education and business in the pursuit of educational improvement. Students can also pursue a Michigan building-level administrator certification through this program.

BUSY LIVES

Blended MA in Educational Studies

This program combines 12 credits of online courses with 18 credits of campus-based courses. Busy students experience both the convenience of self-paced, online learning and the richness of conventional, campus-based courses.

LIFELONG LEARNERS

Teaching and Learning

Students in this program learn about classroom practices, the development of classroom materials, and the ways in which teaching occurs in classrooms. They also discover how theory, research, and practice relate to sound instruction.

FORWARD THINKERS

Program Evaluation and Improvement Research

This program gives students the tools to understand “what works” in education, and how it works. Students learn to apply diverse research methods in order to evaluate educational outcomes and drive continuous improvement in education systems.

LEADERS

Educational Leadership and Policy

This program prepares students to improve schools, instruction, and outcomes. They learn practical leadership skills and gain a theoretical understanding of policy and educational systems. Students with or without a current master’s degree can also pursue a Michigan building-level administrator certification through this program.
